Who are we?

The California Civic Data Coalition is an open-source team of journalists and computer programmers from news organizations across America. Read more »

What do we do?

We create software that makes California's public data easier to analyze. Our current focus is CAL-ACCESS, the database tracking money in state politics. Download and get started »

How can you get involved?

Hundreds of journalists at dozens of news outlets have joined our effort. We want you to be next. Fork us on GitHub, send an e-mail or join our Slack.
